Institutional Overview

Janta Inter College Gangdhari (Khatauli) is a prestigious educational institution committed to delivering high-quality academic instruction to students from Class 6 to Class 12. Our mission is to foster holistic development by combining rigorous academic standards with strong ethical values. Located in a serene environment, we provide the perfect atmosphere for focused learning and growth.

Academic Streams (11th & 12th)

Science Stream

Expert coaching in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. We offer advanced laboratory facilities for practical experimentation.

Commerce Stream

Building future financial leaders through in-depth study of Accountancy, Business Studies, and Economics.

Arts Stream

Developing critical thinking through History, Geography, Political Science, and Literature.

High School

Comprehensive foundation in Science, Mathematics, Social Studies, and Languages (English, Hindi, Sanskrit) for Grades 9 and 10.

Physical Excellence & Games

Outdoor Sports

Indoor Games
